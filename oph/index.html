Core Claim

OPH says it is the fundamental theory of physics.

All other successful theories are effective descriptions of OPH.

OPH starts from local observers on a holographic screen, not from an objective universe with a built-in god's-eye frame. Where observer patches overlap, their descriptions must agree. The laws of physics are the consistency rules that make that agreement possible.

That claim is meant literally. In the OPH view, the Standard Model, special relativity, general relativity, and string theory are all partial, successful, effective descriptions of a deeper structure. OPH further claims to explain why the universe exists at all and why intelligent observers emerge within it.

FAQ

The search terms people actually use.

OPH is aimed directly at theory-of-everything searches, quantum-gravity searches, simulation-theory searches, and the deeper question of why the universe exists and why observers show up inside it.

What is OPH?

Observer Patch Holography is presented as the fundamental theory of physics: reality reconstructed from observer-local patches that must agree on overlap.

Is OPH the literal theory of everything?

Yes. That is the explicit claim: quantum mechanics, general relativity, the Standard Model, and string theory are all effective descriptions of OPH.

Does it explain why the universe exists?

That is part of the pitch. OPH says existence, laws, and observers are not brute facts but consequences of consistency on a holographic screen.

Is this simulation theory?

It overlaps with simulation-theory language, but OPH presents itself as a formal, physics-first version of reality as a simulation-like information process.
