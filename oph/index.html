Observer Patch Holography.

Observer Patch Holography (OPH) is Pragma's concrete implementation of simulation theory and proposed fundamental theory of physics. It models reality as an observer-based fixed-point consensus computation: finite patches compare overlap-visible records, repair mismatch, and converge toward stable public physics. It does not merely place the effective quantum description and gravity in one framework. It explains why the universe exists at all, why it evolves intelligent observers, and why the Standard Model, special relativity, general relativity, and string theory are all effective descriptions of deeper observer-patch structure.

As a simulation theory framework, OPH treats reality as an observer-based fixed-point consensus process reconstructed from local patches on a holographic screen, with explicit synchronization rules and a simulator-hardware story rather than a loose philosophical metaphor.

As a proposed fundamental theory of physics, OPH presents the same fixed-point consensus architecture as a route to physics unification: quantum mechanics, relativity, the Standard Model, and string theory appear as effective descriptions. The global closure of that theory-of-everything claim is the strange-loop hypothesis: reality closes as a self-referential timeless causal structure.

For the direct search-intent entry points, use the simulation theory page for the computational and informational framing, and the theory of everything page for the broader physics-unification claim. For guided learning, start with OPH Textbooks.

No Modest Claims

Everything else is effective description.

OPH is not framed as a niche interpretation, a partial fix, or a speculative side branch. It is the foundational layer. The established theories of modern physics are successful because they compress pieces of OPH structure.

What OPH settles

OPH starts from local observers on a holographic screen. Where patches overlap, their descriptions must agree. Those consistency rules generate the laws of physics. There is no objective view from nowhere and no need to assume a universe with one privileged frame.

That same structure explains why there is a universe at all, why the laws have the shape they do, and why stable intelligent observers emerge inside the system. In OPH's public framing, the theory-of-everything closure is the strange-loop hypothesis: reality closes as a self-referential timeless causal structure.

What becomes derivative

  • The Standard Model is an effective description of observer-patch gluing structure.
  • Special relativity is an effective description of overlap consistency.
  • General relativity is an effective description of entanglement geometry and modular structure.
  • String theory is an effective bridge into deeper OPH edge-sector structure.
  • Simulation theory becomes a concrete mathematical and practical architecture rather than a pop-metaphysics slogan.
  • Physics unification becomes a consequence of one observer-consistency framework rather than a patchwork of separate laws.
  • The theory of everything closes through the strange-loop hypothesis.
