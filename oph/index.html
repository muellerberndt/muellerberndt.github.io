Observer Patch Holography.

Observer Patch Holography (OPH) is Pragma's concrete implementation of simulation theory and proposed fundamental theory of physics. It does not merely unify quantum mechanics and gravity. It explains why the universe exists at all, why it evolves intelligent observers, and why the Standard Model, special relativity, general relativity, and string theory are all effective descriptions of deeper observer-patch structure.

As a simulation theory framework, OPH treats reality as an observer-consistent information process reconstructed from local patches on a holographic screen, with explicit synchronization rules and a simulator-hardware story rather than a loose philosophical metaphor.

As a proposed fundamental theory of physics, OPH presents the same observer-consistency architecture as a route to physics unification: quantum mechanics, relativity, the Standard Model, and string theory emerge as effective descriptions. The global closure of that theory-of-everything claim is the strange-loop hypothesis: reality closes as a self-referential timeless causal structure.

For the direct search-intent entry points, use the simulation theory page for the computational and informational framing, and the theory of everything page for the broader physics-unification claim.

Research status

OPH contains theorem-grade fixed-cutoff results, conditional scaling-limit branches, calibration sectors, and continuation fronts. Several derivations depend on explicit premises, some branches remain open, and the framework should be read with those claim boundaries in view.

What reality is, in OPH

Reality is a computational process built from local observer-perspectives. Those perspectives have to stay mutually consistent where they overlap, so physics is the synchronization law that keeps subjective experiences in agreement.

In OPH, that process runs on a triangulated spherical screen built from qudits. Who built the sphere, and for what? That question is left open here. The material below is the path into it.

No Modest Claims

Everything else is effective description.

OPH is not framed as a niche interpretation, a partial fix, or a speculative side branch. It is the foundational layer. The established theories of modern physics are successful because they compress pieces of OPH structure.

What OPH settles

OPH starts from local observers on a holographic screen. Where patches overlap, their descriptions must agree. Those consistency rules generate the laws of physics. There is no objective view from nowhere and no need to assume a universe with one privileged frame.

That same structure explains why there is a universe at all, why the laws have the shape they do, and why stable intelligent observers emerge inside the system. In OPH's public framing, the theory-of-everything closure is the strange-loop hypothesis: reality closes as a self-referential timeless causal structure.

What becomes derivative

  • The Standard Model is an effective description of observer-patch gluing structure.
  • Special relativity is an effective description of overlap consistency.
  • General relativity is an effective description of entanglement geometry and modular structure.
  • String theory is an effective bridge into deeper OPH edge-sector structure.
  • Simulation theory becomes a concrete mathematical and practical architecture rather than a pop-metaphysics slogan.
  • Physics unification becomes a consequence of one observer-consistency framework rather than a patchwork of separate laws.
  • The theory of everything closes through the strange-loop hypothesis.
